## System 2: Debt Clearance Accelerator

Use the Debt Snowball method with a South African twist:
1. List all debts smallest to largest
2. Pay minimums on everything
3. Throw every extra Rand at the smallest debt
4. When one debt is cleared, roll the payment into the next

Track your “Debt Freedom Date” and review it weekly.

## System 3: Delayed Gratification Engine

Create a simple daily score (1–10):
- Did I stick to my budget today?
- Did I avoid an impulsive purchase?
- Did I do one wealth-building action (content, learning, side income)?

Aim for a weekly average above 7. This compounds faster than any investment return.

## System 4: Rand Volatility Hedge

Simple rule for 2026:
- Keep 60–80% in ZAR-based assets (emergency fund, local investing)
- Allocate 20–40% to offshore/USD assets (global index funds, USD cash, or stablecoins where appropriate)
- Rebalance only when ZAR strengthens or weakens more than 15% from your baseline.

This protects purchasing power during both Rand crashes and unexpected strengthening.
